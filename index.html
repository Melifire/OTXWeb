Who are we?

The purpose of Theta Tau is to develop and maintain a high standard of professional interest among its members, and to unite them in a strong bond of fraternal fellowship.

Theta Tau is a professional co-ed engineering fraternity dedicated to the advancement of its members in their personal and professional lives.

Our Pillars

At Chi chapter, we have three pillars. These are areas of life we motivate and help our members to express themselves in.

Brotherhood

We believe a community of likeminded people to be a strong base for ones life. Here at Chi Chapter, you'll find Engineers and Computer Scientists who will help you reach your goals and support you.

Philanthropy

As engineers, we strive to make the world around us better. At Chi chapter, we give back to our community through blood drives, road cleanings, and more.

Professional Development

As a professional fraternity, we help eachother develop our professional skills such as resume writing and general skills. We also give countless combined years of experience in engineering and computer science positions and organizations.
